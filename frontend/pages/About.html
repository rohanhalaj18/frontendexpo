About TechStore

We're passionate about bringing you the finest electronic components and development kits. Our mission is to empower developers, engineers, and tech enthusiasts with premium quality products that fuel innovation.

Our Story

Every semester, freshers spend thousands on Exploration kits they’ll only use once. At the same time, seniors are left with perfectly good kits gathering dust. That’s when we decided to step in. We began collecting these kits, stocking them, and making them available for the next batch of freshers at affordable prices. That’s how ExploroTech was born—built by students, for students, to make engineering exploration smarter and more sustainable

Our Mission

To make engineering labs lighter on the pocket, we provide freshers with affordable, ready-to-use lab kits—sourced from seniors, carefully stocked by us, and quality-checked to ensure every component works. At ExploroTech, you save money, reduce waste, and get kits you can rely on.
